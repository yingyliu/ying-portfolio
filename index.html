About Me

Hi, I’m Ying Ying! I’m from Taipei. I started a business in tourism because I have a strong passion for helping people and wanted to inspire others to start traveling and exploring the world, too. After many years of trying to find a job that reflected my love for traveling, I decided to take matters into my own hands and start my own business.
